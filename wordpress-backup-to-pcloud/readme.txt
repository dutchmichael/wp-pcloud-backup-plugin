Keep your valuable WordPress website, its media and data backed up to pCloud in minutes with this cool and user-friendly plugin.

== Description ==

[WordPress Backup to pCloud](http://www.pcloud.com) has been created to backup your blog and its data.

Just choose a day, time and how often you wish your backup to be performed and your database to be dropped in your pCloud!

Checkout the website - http://www.pcloud.com
